How to Buy

It is really simple to bid in our auction

STEP 1: REGISTER

  • You need to register and complete the verification process.
  • You need to create your login id and password.
  • Accept the terms and conditions of the auction.
  • Upload one proof of identity issued by the government.
  • In certain cases you might be asked to make a deposit.
  • On successful completion of the registration and verification you will have access to bid.

STEP 2: CHOOSE YOUR BIDDING METHOD

Bid in the Room

This is the most exciting part of the auction! Where you will be an active bidder in the room, just walk in with the identity document you have registered with and collect your paddle prior to the start of the auction.

Place an Advance Bid

Prior commitments don’t allow for you be in the saleroom, you can still participate in the auction by placing an “advance bid”, which you can do online. What’s more is that your bid value would be recorded in an encrypted format to maintain the sanctity of your bid.

Use the Telephone

You can actively participate in the auction as a phone bidder. Please register for phone bidding and we will call you during the auction and place bids on your behalf

Bid Online

Our online bidding platform streams a live video of the auction and you can bid from your computer from anywhere in the world.

How do I pay for my purchases?

If you are a successful bidder you will receive an email immediately at the close of the auction confirming the same.

You will receive a proforma invoice with the hammer value, the applicable taxes and the shipping and handling charges.

Payment should be completed within 7 days from the receipt of the proforma invoice.

  • RTGS
  • Cheque/ Demand Draft

How do I receive my purchase?

On completion of payment you can choose from the following options :

1. You will be able to collect the works from our offices if the sale has taken place in either New Delhi or Mumbai

2. We will ship the work to a shipping address provided by you (please refer to the T&C of the sale for more details)
